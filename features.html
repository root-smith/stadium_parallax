TICKETING

  • Bleachr’s ticketing platform provides a central hub for ticketing through an innovative and intuitive user experience all self-contained within the App.
  • Integrate API’s from team’s base ticketing platforms (API required)
  • Friends & group mobile ticket transfers
  • Allows for rewards points integration for ticket purchases
  • Day-of-event Access Pass sales through the app is a way to both increase utilization of VIP seating and tent areas and provide fans with special viewing and experience opportunities
  • Ticket upgrade opportunities with push notification and in-app messaging
  • Dynamic pricing

IN-SEAT ORDERING

  • Our ordering module is extremely flexible and developed specifically to work with zero hardware requirements
  • Rapid delivery without interrupting play
  • Significant value can be provided to fans in large grandstand areas where fans are often in the same seat for hours
  • Runners receive orders on their phones and deliver to fans in just minutes
  • Fans don’t need to leave their seats and worry about long lines at concession stands or their seat being taken upon return in the case of golf grandstands
  • Simple coolers and folding stocking tables are all that is needed to store product
  • Stadium POS integration available as extra option
  • Over 1200 orders of just beer and water were processed this way for Tennis Canada’s 2017 Rogers Cup

BEYOND F&B:

In-Seat Merchandise Delivery

  • Eliminates fan frustration with long lines at Merchandise stands
  • Opportunity for in-seat delivery or a “ship-to-my-home” option if fans don’t want the inconvenience of carrying a bag around an event.
  • A “ship-to-my-home” option within our existing store module in the app alleviates these issues and still allows the fan to have the emotional connection of having purchased the merchandise while at the event
  • Particularly relevant when there are long gaps between play or weather delays when special offers can be pushed through the app

EVENT NAVIGATION

Provide GPS-accurate markers of different areas of interest, including:

Traffic on Site:

  • Develop an overlay heatmap on the Map showing hotspots of each high traffic area
  • A map layer that can be toggled showing estimated wait times for each supporting marker

Shop Information:

  • Provide a list view of all shops at the event, with filtering and searching based on name and category
  • User will be able to toggle between a list view and map view displaying only shops
  • Will provide a detailed information screen for each shop

Travel Information:

  • General list view with links to 3rd party transportation and travel information web sites; which when clicked, would open up inside the application

AR-LOCATION WAYFINDER

  • Bleachr is currently developing a wayfinding module that makes use of the latest AR technology to guide a fan to a desired destination while giving the team or venue the ability to include sponsor integrations, special offers and notable points-of-interest along the way
  • Efficient pathways to user destinations can be developed to assist with crowd flow and congestion mitigation
  • Wayfinding uses a fan’s phone to captures the immediate surroundings
  • The image of the immediate surroundings is instantaneously matched against pre-recorded video to determine fan’s location in-or-around the venue
  • Fan selects desired destination and is guided to it via an intuitive magic arrow that appears on the user’s phone screen

  • 360 VR provides a great premium revenue opportunity and can be experienced using a “magic window” directly on a mobile device or with 360 VR goggles for a totally immersive experience
  • Compatible with various cameras

NOTIFICATIONS

Bleachr has an existing module to handle both push notifications and socket-based notifications

Socket-based notifications:

  • Significant percentage of users will not grant push notification permissions
  • Socket based connections save power compared to wake and poll systems
  • Scale: Socket based communication has less load on the server than broad- based HTTP

Auto/System-generated notifications:

  • Follow-a-player: events triggered by players such as teeing off on first hole or player’s tennis match is starting
  • Weather: delay and start times

Manual notifications:

  • Parking lot closures
  • Concession stand promotions
  • Player autographs

  • This will include reminder focused local notifications, as well as in-app overlay reminders
  • Collaborate with Wifi partner to ensure a positive user experience
